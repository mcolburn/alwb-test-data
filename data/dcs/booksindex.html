Sacraments and Services

Links with the label PDF/Print open the pdf files of the texts, which were previously available on the eMatins website.

Links with the label Text/Music open the html text of a service, which has the links to scores and audio. To open the score of a particular hymn or listen to the audio recording if available, scroll to the desired place in the service, click the Byzantine neum or the Western note for the respective score, or the audio icon for the recording. To print a score that you have opened, use your device's file print options.
